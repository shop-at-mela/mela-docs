## Design Recommendations

1. **One idea per slide** - current deck violates this
2. **≤50 words of prose per slide** - use bullets, tables, diagrams
3. **Consistent visual style** - headers, fonts, colors
4. **Add visuals:**
   - Product category images (Slide 5)
   - Platform screenshot (Slide 6)
   - Simple flow diagrams (Slide 6)
   - Team photo (Slide 11)
5. **Use icons** for checkmarks, X marks, arrows
